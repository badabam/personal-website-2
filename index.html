Biography

I was born in February 1990 in Erlangen and ever since have preferred the unconventional way. That's why I chose a humanistic high school and learned Latin and Ancient Greek, or why I went for a career as a blogger for more than 10 years.
I moved to Hamburg in 2014 and did many different kinds of jobs but deep in my heart, I always knew I need to do something creative.

In 7th grade, I took part of an elective course in IT and that's when I first fell in love with coding - but it took me 18 more years to actually choose this way.

As a kid, I created my first websites on Beepworld, and as a teenager, I used Blogspot to create a few blogs, before heading into the fun world of Wordpress.

In 2020, I finally decided to become a Web Developer and am currently attending a remote bootcamp with neue fische, so I can start my new path and be a successful Frontend Developer.
